This repository currently contains a survey of public-cloud and neo-cloud offerings that are useful when you care about forever-free compute, free credits, and practical GPU access.

This is a point-in-time snapshot verified on 2026-04-16, not a continuously maintained database.

Current document:

- [Free cloud and GPU offers in 2026: forever-free tiers, trial credits, and practical loopholes](research/free-cloud-and-gpu-offers-2026-04.md)

The research emphasizes:

- what is actually free versus just marketed as free
- which offers matter for CPU and GPU workloads
- where startup, student, and research status unlocks much larger benefits
- constraints, anti-abuse rules, and pricing traps that can make an offer far less useful than it looks
